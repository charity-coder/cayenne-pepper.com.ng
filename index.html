Who We Are

Nigeria's Premier Pepper Farmers Network

The Cayenne Pepper Farmers Association of Nigeria (CAPFAN) is the leading professional body representing the interests of cayenne pepper farmers, processors, and traders across the federation.

We were founded in Nigeria with a mandate to transform Nigeria's cayenne pepper sector through collective action, modern agricultural practices, and market linkages.

Our People

Meet the Leadership

Dedicated professionals steering CAPFAN's mission to empower Nigeria's cayenne pepper farmers.

National President
HON OGOENYI AKUEJIOWU IKECHUKWU
National President Agriculturist Policy Advocate

Hon. Ogoenyi Akuejiowu Ikechukwu is the founding National President of CAPFAN, elected by acclamation at the association's inaugural congress in 2022. A seasoned agribusiness leader and community mobiliser, he has spent over two decades championing the rights and livelihoods of smallholder farmers across the Niger Delta and South-East geopolitical zones.

Under his visionary leadership, CAPFAN has grown from a fledgling idea into Nigeria's most recognised cayenne pepper advocacy body — with a membership spanning all 36 states and the FCT. Hon. Ikechukwu personally spearheaded CAPFAN's landmark partnership with the Federal Ministry of Industry, Trade and Investment, securing the association's National Secretariat at the Old Federal Secretariat Complex, Garki, Abuja.

His guiding philosophy — "When the farmer prospers, the nation prospers" — continues to inspire CAPFAN's work across value chain development, capacity building, and international market linkages.
